Annual Productions:

Pagal Hai Kya!!

Our Play "Pagal Hai Kya!" follows the journey of a child exploring his differences from other children, encountering challenges and unique individuals who help him understand and embrace those differences. The play aims to dispel misconceptions about autism spectrum disorder, highlighting that it is a neurological condition, not a disease, and can be managed with care and patience.
